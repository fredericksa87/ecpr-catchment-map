Methodology

OHCA Incidence: 110 per 100,000 population/year (CARES 2024 registry, EMS-assessed non-traumatic)

Shockable Rhythm: 18.1% of OHCA present with VF/pVT (CARES 2024)

Age Filter (18-75): ~83% of shockable OHCA fall within 18-75 age range

ECPR Eligibility: ~15-17% of shockable, age-eligible OHCA are refractory candidates

Activation Goal: Conservative estimate based on 15-19% of eligible candidates meeting full refractory criteria

Catchment Radius: 45-minute ground EMS transport (~17 mi / 27 km straight-line radius, accounting for road routing factor)

Population: NCTCOG 2025 estimates for all cities within radius
